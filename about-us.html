Aamantran Welfare Association

Aamantran Welfare Association is made up of collectively believed thoughts, that everyone has the right to live a positive and healthy life. The name Aamantran is so vibrant to invite everyone as family becasue we all know Bond is stronger than blood and the family grows stronger by bond. We are experienced in working with society and people who have the most complex and chaotic lives; enabling and empowering them to take charge of themselves, giving them the skills and resilience to live healthy and fulfilled lives.

About Aamantran

Doing More with less

Since our start, Aamantran has grown from the living room table where we first began. Founded as a cultural activity specifically for the society and social sectors, our team was built knowing that here, we need to do more with less. We are like-minded and passionate individuals eager to contribute to a better future. Our team is always discovering new ways to help our people make an even greater impact.

Our values

Eager to succeed and to accomplish as much as possible for our people. We value openness in all our communication and believe that a transparent approach to workplace interactions results in the best outcomes for our society and for our families.

Our objectives:

Achieve the goal of togetherness - We need to ensure that non of the negative thought barriers will distract us from our main purpose of coming together with joining hands to lead fulfilling and independent lives.

Our vision:

To create a society in which vulnerable people have the opportunity to live fulfilled, healthy and constructive lives

Our Empowerment

Our experience in joining hands and provide a safe environment in which all of us are supported to reach their greatest potential, have choice and control over their own development and have fulfilled, healthy and constructive lives.

Our Strength

We come up with a wide spectrum of support to each other and have the tenacity to succeed with challenging ,complex and in tough situations.Our strength is in our culture, diversity and equality of opportunities.
